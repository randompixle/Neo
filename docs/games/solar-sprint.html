Movement tech

Solar Sprint borrows the punchy physics of Carlson 2D: heavy gravity, aggressive acceleration, and a short-but-satisfying dash. Chaining boosts lets advanced runners maintain dash speed through the course.

  • Sprint: Hold A/D or the arrow buttons to accelerate.
  • Jump: Tap W/↑ or the jump glyph. Tap again mid-air for a double jump.
  • Dash: Press Shift (or ⚡) to surge forward. Use energy nodes to instantly refresh the dash.
  • Hazards: Red panels reset your run. Touch the glowing exit pillar to log your time.
