Solar Sprint

Blaze across a floating test facility, chaining dashes and precise jumps to shave milliseconds off your best clear time. Energy nodes reset your dash mid-air, hazards send you back to the start, and the glowing exit pillar records personal bests automatically.

Controls & tips

  • Use A / D or ← / → to sprint. Tap the on-screen arrows when playing on a phone.
  • Tap W, ↑, or the jump button to leap. A quick follow-up grants a double jump.
  • Hit Shift (or the dash icon) to burst forward. Collect energy nodes to refresh the dash mid-air.
  • Hazards reset the run. Reach the radiant exit pillar to lock in your time and update your personal best.
  • Your fastest finish is stored locally so you can chase new records every visit.
